Welcome to Llamarama: A Quest For More Gold the super-rad llama story-search-question-dialogue based adventure game!

In Llamarama: A Quest For More Gold you are a llama. You are trying to find gold. Lots of gold. So you must quest. During your quest you come across a wise soul who may know where gold is. Turns out it does. In fact, the soul has gold. However, the soul will not part with the gold until you answer its riddle. Right. But there is a twist. If you do not answer the riddle wisely enough the soul will mug you and loot your gold. Bummer. Your a pretty poor llama so that is not ideal.

Try to be a smart llama. Win your gold. Or you will be mugged--hard.

Obviously, you are dying to play.

But first lets go over the rules of the game:

Rules

  • Enter words into the text boxes
  • Use you llama-logic to solve the mysterious riddle
  • The better your llama-logic is the more gold you will gain
  • The worse your llama-logic is and you will probably be mugged
